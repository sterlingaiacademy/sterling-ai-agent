Setup Guide

Everything you need to configure Sterling AI — clearly explained, step by step.

"The best investment you can make is in tools that work while you sleep."
— Your future self, thanking you

Yes, this setup looks like a lot. But here's the truth: you only do this once. After today, your Sterling AI agent handles emails, meetings, expenses, and web searches — all from WhatsApp. Every minute you spend here saves you hours every week. Let's build your personal AI, one step at a time. 🚀

🧑‍💻

Step 1 — Create a Meta Developer App

Set up your Meta developer account and create the WhatsApp-enabled app

▾
1

Go to developers.facebook.com and sign in with your personal Facebook account.

2

Click My Apps → Create App. When asked for the use case, select "Business" and click Next.

3

Give your app a name (e.g. Sterling AI Agent), enter your email, and optionally link a Meta Business Account. Click Create App.

4

In your app dashboard, scroll down to find WhatsApp and click Set Up. This adds WhatsApp as a product to your app.

5

Under WhatsApp → Getting Started, you'll see a test phone number and a Phone Number ID. Copy and save this — you'll need it in the setup form.

💡 Tip: You don't need to publish your app. It works in Development mode for your own number. Just make sure your WhatsApp number is added as a test recipient.
🔑

Step 2 — Get Your Permanent WhatsApp Token

Create a System User token that never expires

▾
⚠️ Important: The temporary token on the Getting Started page expires in 24 hours. You MUST follow these steps to get a permanent token that works forever.
1

Go to business.facebook.com/settings and make sure you're inside your Meta Business Suite.

2

In the left menu, go to Users → System Users. Click Add to create a new System User. Name it (e.g. "Sterling AI Bot") and set role to Admin.

3

After creating the System User, click Add Assets. Select Apps, choose your app (Sterling AI Agent), and grant it Full Control.

4

Click Generate New Token on the System User. Select your app. Under permissions, check whatsapp_business_messaging and whatsapp_business_management. Click Generate Token.

5

Copy the token immediately — it is only shown once. This is your Permanent Access Token. Paste it into the setup form above.

💡 Tip: This token is tied to the System User, not a personal account. It will not expire unless you manually revoke it. Keep it private — treat it like a password.
🔗

Step 3 — Configure Webhook & Verify Token

Connect your Sterling AI server to receive WhatsApp messages

▾
1

In your Meta app dashboard, go to WhatsApp → Configuration. Click Edit next to the Webhook section.

2

In the Callback URL field, enter your Render server URL followed by /webhook/whatsapp. Example:

https://your-app-name.onrender.com/webhook/whatsapp
3

In the Verify Token field, type exactly: my_verify_token

This exact value is already pre-filled in the setup form. It's a secret handshake between Meta and your server.

4

Click Verify and Save. Meta will call your server to confirm it's running. If it fails, make sure your Render deployment is live.

5

After saving, click Manage next to Webhook Fields and subscribe to messages. This makes Meta send all WhatsApp messages to your server.

⚠️ Keep in mind: The verify token must match exactly — my_verify_token — case-sensitive, no spaces. If you change it in the form, update it in Meta too.
💡 Tip: If Render is sleeping (free tier), send one request to your app URL first to wake it up, then verify the webhook in Meta.
🔥

Step 4 — Get Your Fireflies API Key

Enable AI-powered online meeting recording and transcription

▾
1

Visit app.fireflies.ai and sign up for a free account using your email.

2

Once logged in, click your profile icon (top right) → Settings.

3

In the left menu, scroll down to find the API Access section. Click Generate API Key.

4

Copy the key and paste it into the Fireflies API Key field in the setup form.

💡 What it does: When you share a Google Meet, Zoom, or Teams link in WhatsApp, Sterling AI sends a bot to join, record, and transcribe your meeting automatically. The free tier gives 800 mins/month — more than enough to start.
🤖

Step 5 — Get Your OpenAI API Key

Power the brain behind Sterling AI — GPT-4o + Whisper

▾
1

Go to platform.openai.com and sign in or create a free account.

2

Click your profile icon (top right) → API Keys → Create new secret key. Give it a name like "Sterling AI".

3

Copy the key (it starts with sk-proj-...) and paste it into the OpenAI API Key field in the setup form.

4

You'll need to add a small credit (minimum $5) to your OpenAI account at platform.openai.com/settings/billing. Sterling AI costs roughly $0.01–$0.05 per conversation — very affordable.

⚠️ Keep your key private: Never share this key. If exposed, revoke it immediately from the OpenAI dashboard and generate a new one.
💡 Tip: You can set usage limits in OpenAI settings so you never get charged more than you expect. Go to Settings → Limits and set a monthly cap.
📊

Step 6 — Set Up Your Google Sheet

Create the finance tracking ledger connected to your agent

▾
1

Go to sheets.google.com with the same Google account you connected in Step 1 (Google Auth).

2

Create a new spreadsheet. In Row 1, add these exact column headers:

Date  |  Credit/Debit  |  Purpose  |  Amount  |  Balance
3

Look at the URL of your sheet. The Sheet ID is the long string between /d/ and /edit:

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/THIS_IS_YOUR_SHEET_ID/edit
4

Copy that ID and paste it into the Finance Tracking Sheet ID field in the setup form.

💡 Tip: You don't need to share the sheet with anyone. Sterling AI accesses it directly using your Google credentials that were authorized in Step 1.
⚠️ Keep in mind: Make sure the sheet is in the same Google account you used for Google Auth. Cross-account access will not work.
🎉

You're Almost There!

Once you fill in the setup form and hit Save & Deploy Agent, your Sterling AI is live. From that point on, just open WhatsApp and talk to your agent — it handles the rest. No apps, no dashboards, just intelligence at your fingertips.
